What is the correct way to apply Beard Oil?

Apply Beard Oil by putting a few drops of oil in the palm of your hand and rubbing them together. Run your hands through your beard working the oil in, then comb through your beard to evenly distribute the oil throughout your hair and to the skin underneath.
